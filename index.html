Glass Selection

We recommend specific glass types by scene and performance requirement.

Tempered Glass
3C/SGCC certified safety glass.
LOW-E Glass
Bidirectional heat regulation: blocks summer solar gain and retains winter heat. Different configurations to meet U-value and/or Solar Heat Gain Coefficient (SHGC) requirements.
Insulated (IGU)
Double/triple glazed with LOW-E option. Dramatically improves sound and thermal insulation.
Laminated Glass
Suitable for sound-sensitive, and high-rise, high-grade curtain wall doors and windows applications.
4SG Premium Glass
25-year warranty. Thermoplastic spacer, ≥80% argon retention. Full-frequency noise reduction.
Decorative / Privacy
Tinted, frosted, PDLC smart glass, silk-screen, and more. Available for applicable product types.
