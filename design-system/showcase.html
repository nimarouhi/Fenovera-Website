Signature Treatments

Brand · Private Showcase Only

Three restrained proposals for visual distinctiveness. No gradients, blobs, or generic AI patterns. All demos are private showcase only — not on any public page until explicitly approved.

Treatment A — Architectural Mullion Grid
76 Series · Casement & Swing
Precision framing, architectural scale

The grid motif echoes fenestration engineering — a structural, functional pattern that grounds hero sections without decorative excess.

Implementation: repeating-linear-gradient at 40px interval, color-border-subtle, pointer-events:none overlay. Subtle at 1px, strong at 1.5px. Avoid above 1.5px.
Treatment B — Vertical Bronze Rule
Technical Authority
Every specification is independently sourced. Every installation detail, confirmed.
152 Series
Lift & Slide Doors
Implementation: border-left: 3px solid var(--color-bronze). Use for pull-quotes, product intros, callout paragraphs. 3px maximum width. Never use on more than 2 elements per section.
Treatment C — Series Numeral Watermark
76
76 Series
Casement & Swing Windows

The series number as background element reinforces product identity without interfering with readability. Opacity 4%, color-navy.

Implementation: position:absolute pseudo-element, font-size 200px, font-weight 800, opacity .04, color-navy. Right-anchored, centered vertically. Only on product hero cards, not on page-wide sections. Never stack two numerals.

ARIA & Keyboard
  • Trigger: aria-haspopup="true", aria-expanded toggles true/false, aria-controls points to panel id
  • Panel: role="region" with aria-label matching the trigger text

ARIA & Keyboard
  • Drawer: role="dialog", aria-modal="true", aria-label="Navigation menu"

Keyboard Behavior
  • Enter / Space toggles panel. Tab moves to next trigger or into open panel content.
  • Home / End: moves focus to first/last trigger in the group.
  • Trigger: aria-expanded true/false, aria-controls = panel id. Panel: role="region", aria-labelledby = trigger id.
  • Panels use hidden attribute (not visibility/opacity) to remove from accessibility tree when closed.

Keyboard Behavior

Content · Dialog-based
Accessibility & Keyboard
  • Gallery thumbnails are <button> elements with aria-label="View larger: [description]"
  • Lightbox uses native <dialog>: showModal() opens with built-in focus trap and backdrop.

  • Lightbox aria-label="Image lightbox" and aria-modal="true" prevent background from being announced.
